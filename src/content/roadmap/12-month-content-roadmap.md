# VaultaCloud — 12-Month Content Publishing Roadmap
## January 2025 – December 2025

---

## Strategy Overview

### Primary Goals
1. Rank page 1 for "cloud storage Nigeria", "private cloud Africa", "offline file storage Nigeria" and 15+ related keywords within 12 months
2. Establish VaultaCloud as the definitive resource on file management for African businesses
3. Generate organic inbound leads from Google Search and AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini)
4. Build domain authority through consistent long-form content and comparison pages

### Content Pillars
- **Pillar 1:** Private Cloud Storage (core product content)
- **Pillar 2:** Offline File Management (primary differentiator)
- **Pillar 3:** African Business Technology (market context)
- **Pillar 4:** Industry Use Cases (vertical content)
- **Pillar 5:** Data Ownership & Sovereignty (thought leadership)
- **Pillar 6:** SME Productivity (educational)

### Publishing Cadence
- **Blog:** 2 articles/week (8–10/month)
- **LinkedIn:** 3 posts/week (12/month)
- **Twitter/X:** 1 post/day (30/month)
- **Comparison pages:** 1 new page/month
- **Documentation:** Continuous (1–2 new pages/month)
- **Case studies:** 1 per quarter
